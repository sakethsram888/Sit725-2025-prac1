The You Yangs National Park is a dog-friendly park located between Melbourne and Geelong.

You Yangs Regional Parkclose

The best walk to try (for you and your pupper) is the 3.2-kilometre Flinders Peak Walk, which takes you to the highest point of the You Yangs. It’ll certainly have you puffing, with about 450 steps and an elevation rise of about 200 metres, but you’ll feel like a proper champ once you reach the top.
